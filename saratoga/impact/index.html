Below is the list of atoms and molecules defined in the design system. Click here for some background on our process or to better understand how the different parts of our structure work together.

Atoms Download File

button

Button styles to ensure proper row alignment.

figcaption

Default styles for all media captions.

figure

Semantic wrapper for media in stories and cards.

h1

Primary headline on story pages, video pages and video playlists. Lead headline on homepage and section pages.

h2

Secondary headlines in the lead group on the homepage and section pages. Can also be used in the story body.

h3

Used in headline stacks and cards on the homepage and section pages. Can also be used in a story.

h4

Used as footer labels on all pages. Can also be used in a story.

h5

Used as labels for headline stacks. Can also be used in a story.

h6

Used primarily for kickers. Can also be used in a story.

label

Labels for the tops of cards and headline stacks on section pages.

lists

Default styles for lists in story bodies.

more-link

Used below grid digests.

p

Default text on story pages.

summary

Summary text used in cards and headline stacks.

time

Used for publish and modified times.

Molecules Download File

card

Enhanced paper molecule with flex display.

card-info

Provides spacing for test in cards and digests.

impact

Reverse impact on any element.

paper

Default paper molecule with a block display.

Organisms

digest

Headline stacks for section content.
